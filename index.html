About Children Life English Boarding School

Children Life English Boarding School is a private school located in Baglung Municipality, Baglung district, within Gandaki province. It provides education from Early Childhood Development (ECD) to Grade 10, with plans to expand to Grade 12. As of 2081 IEMIS report, CLEBS has 1238 students enrolled.

Message from the Chairperson

Chairperson, School Management Committee

Building Futures with Purpose

At Children Life English Boarding School (CLEBS), we believe that education is the foundation of a better future. Our vision is to provide a nurturing and disciplined environment where children can grow not just academically, but also morally, emotionally, and socially.

As the chairperson, I am proud of the progress we’ve made and the values we uphold. We strive to blend traditional education with modern methods, ensuring our students are well-equipped for the challenges of the future. I invite you to be part of the CLEBS family and join us in shaping responsible, confident, and compassionate individuals.
